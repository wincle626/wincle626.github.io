Welcome Ms. Yanjing Zhang to visit Queen's University Belfast !

Yanjing Zhang is currently pursuing her Ph.D. at the School of Integrated Circuits, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA). Her research focuses on approximation-based optimization for communication system receivers, including channel estimation and approximate polar decoder design. Her interests lie in developing high-performance and low-power communication architectures for modern wireless systems.
